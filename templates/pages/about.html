About FoodLoop

Connecting communities to reduce food waste and fight hunger.

Our Mission

FoodLoop is dedicated to reducing food waste while addressing food insecurity in our communities. We provide a simple, efficient platform for food donors to connect with those in need, ensuring that surplus food reaches people instead of landfills.

The Problem We're Solving

Every year, billions of pounds of food go to waste while millions of people struggle with food insecurity. This disconnect hurts our environment, wastes resources, and leaves vulnerable community members without access to nutritious meals.

FoodLoop bridges this gap by making it easy for restaurants, grocery stores, and individuals to share surplus food with local recipients who need it most.

How We Do It

1

Simple Posting

Donors list surplus food with pickup details and expiry information.

2

Smart Matching

Recipients can search and filter based on dietary needs and preferences.

3

Direct Connection

Users coordinate pickup times and complete exchanges directly.

4

Community Trust

Rating system ensures quality and accountability for all users.
