How FoodLoop Works

Three simple steps to make a real difference in your community

1. Join the Community

Sign up as a donor or recipient. Complete your profile and verify your email to start making an impact.

  • Quick registration
  • Email verification
  • Profile setup

2. Share or Discover

Donors post available food with photos. Recipients browse, search, or use our map to find donations.

  • Photo uploads
  • Smart search
  • Interactive map

3. Connect & Collect

Coordinate pickup through our platform. Complete the cycle and help reduce food waste in Kenya.

  • Instant notifications
  • Pickup coordination
  • Success tracking
